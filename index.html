Osamu Shibuya :

Computer Science Major, Art Minor


Osamu Shibuya, originally from Japan, is currently a senior at NDSU pursuing a major in Computer Science with a minor in Arts. His academic journey took an interesting turn after spending three years immersed in the world of visual arts, where his passion for drawing flourished. However, his fascination with understanding the inner workings of applications and websites led him to pivot towards computer science. Over the past two years, Osamu has delved deep into the realms of coding and programming, finding joy in the process of unraveling the mysteries behind familiar. He relishes the challenge of learning new technologies and applying them creatively, drawing from both his artistic sensibilities and technical expertise.
